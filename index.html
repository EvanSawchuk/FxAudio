Welcome to FX Audio, your premier destination for high-quality car audio and electronics.

  • Car Audio
  • Mobile Video

  • GPS Navigation & Tracking

We offer products from top brands for high-fidelity car audio/video systems. Whether you're looking for a simple upgrade or a custom installation fit, our team can handle projects of any size, limited only by your imagination or budget.

Beyond automotive solutions, we also provide custom home theater installations, multi-room audio, HD video distribution, and computer networking for both residential and commercial properties. We handle new construction as well as retrofitting older homes and buildings. As full CEDIA members and accredited Level 1 Installers, we guarantee top-tier service.

Our mission is to enhance our customers' lives by making their everyday vehicles exciting, convenient, and safe. We achieve this through constantly evolving our installation techniques, the latest 12-volt technology, and a friendly, educational, and trustworthy environment.
